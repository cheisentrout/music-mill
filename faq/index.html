Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session?

Contact our staff and inquire about our availability. We usually book up 3-5 months in advance, so it's a good idea to plan ahead.

What do I need to bring with me to record at Music Mill?

Not much! We provide all necessary accomodations, recording equipment, and instruments. We even provide snacks!

How much does it cost to record with you?

It depends! We bundle our services separately for individual projects - everyone needs something a little different. Reach out to our studio staff to collaborate about what pricing might look like for you and your project.
